question: What will the puppies come with?
---

All puppies are vet checked, microchipped, up to date on shots and dewormer, and come with a puppy basket containing:

- goodies
- their microchip pamphlet
- a contract copy
- vet records
- a blanket smelling like home
- a "raising a puppy" guide written by us
- their tiny newborn collars so you can see how much they have grown
