question: How can we pick one when we haven't met them yet?
---

Schedule a video call with us to meet and pick your pup.

Before they are old enough to fully show their individual personalities, you can still feel confident in a great outcome: they are purebred golden retrievers and will display golden retriever traits - happy-go-lucky, sweet, snuggly, and goofy.

There will still be individual differences. One pup might be more barky, another more brave, another more observant. One might sleep on their back, and another may want to be king of the snuggle pile. That is all okay.
