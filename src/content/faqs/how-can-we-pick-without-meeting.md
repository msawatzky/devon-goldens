order: 3
question: How can we pick one when we haven't met them yet?
---

You place a deposit before 4 weeks of age, then pick your puppy on pick day (around 4–5 weeks) in order of deposit date. By then, their individual personalities are starting to show, so you can choose the pup that feels like the best fit for your family.

Until pick day, you can still feel confident in a great outcome: they are purebred golden retrievers and will display golden retriever traits - happy-go-lucky, sweet, snuggly, and goofy.

There will still be individual differences. One pup might be more barky, another more brave, another more observant. One might sleep on their back, and another may want to be king of the snuggle pile. That is all okay.
