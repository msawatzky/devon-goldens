question: What can you tell us about their health?
---

At the seven-week vet check, puppies receive their first round of shots (part of a set of three), are up to date on dewormer, and are microchipped.

For microchips, puppies are registered to us as primary owners to help prevent them from ending up in shelters, and new owners are registered as secondary.

Both parents have a clean bill of health and are up to date on all requirements.

Anything that happens while the puppy is with us, or is caused by their stay with us, is covered by us.
